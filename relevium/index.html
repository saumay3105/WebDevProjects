Mission Statement
“To change is to innovate.”

We envision a world in which the most vulnerable people will have the power to speak for themselves. A world where the most destitute of children to the richest are the ones who reshape the world. The crux of our work lies in our ability to “nourish and assist”, whether it be a small seedling or a child studying under the lamp. The whole essence of team Relevium lies in the English translation of our venture, ”Relief”.

Lets give the unfortunate a chance.
